About Me

Dan Gabel is a Lighting Designer and aspiring Full Stack Developer living in Chicago. He wakes up every morning to Mulder, his chuggle (chihuahua, beagle, pug mix). A mutt if there has ever been one.


On a typical day, Dan can be found doing anything from installing large sound and lighting systems in nightclubs to writing plugins for GrandMA2. Lighting has been a large part of Dan's life since high school, where he lit the school plays.


His favorite hobbies include replaying NES classics and arguing with his friends about who is the top heel on Monday Night Raw.
